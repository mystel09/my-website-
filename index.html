Ghost - The letter game you play with friends!

Ghost, is a word game where you try to get your friends to spell words from the letters you input. Keep the word going as long as you can. If you finish a word before the other players you lose!
- Play locally with up to 4 players
- Play online with up to 2 players
- Challenge your friends when you think they can't spell a word
- Gain a richer vocabulary as you try to expand on your words
- Think in a fast environment where all sessions are timed
*You must have a Game Center account to play online.
